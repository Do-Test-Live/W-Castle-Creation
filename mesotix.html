德國及以色列合作研發Mesotix

Mesotix 由德國及以色列合作研發的全新專利儀器， 使用嶄新TMA 純熱能傳輸技術，以毫秒之間的速度將攝氏400度高溫傳送至皮膚的真皮層。在皮膚開創成千上萬的微細管道，注入精華素來刺激骨膠原的增生，即時提昇輪廓達至V面、抗皺、均勻膚色的效果。

Mesotix 以先進技術為美容中心而設的皮膚重 塑美容儀器。療程原理通過鈦金屬合成小金字塔，加熱至攝氏400度，以專利TMA技術刺激皮膚細胞的再生和修復，促進膠原蛋白和彈性蛋白的生成，改善皮膚的彈性和光滑度。使用Mesotix疼感極低，可不需鎮痛藥膏，而且不發出任何輻射只採用純自然熱、癒合快和停機時間短。 Mesotix採用無菌鈦尖作為小金字塔形針腳且易於清潔,而且在加熱程序中進行消毒。

Mesotix 療程非入侵性，復原期極短，能適合任何類型皮膚問題的顧客使用。

Mesotix 可配合特選精華一起使用，精華可以根據不同的皮膚問題和治療目的而定制，能夠更好地滲透到皮膚深層，以達到更好的治療效果，改善多種皮膚問題，例如細紋、皺紋、暗沉、松弛、痘疤和毛孔粗大等。更可以改善多個區域，例如面部、頸部、肩部、手部、手臂和身體。

Mesotix is a new patented instrument developed through a collaboration between Germany and Israel for use in beauty centers. It uses a revolutionary TMA pure thermal energy transfer technology to transmit temperatures of up to 400 degrees Celsius to the dermis of the skin in just milliseconds. This creates thousands of tiny channels in the skin, allowing for the injection of essence to stimulate collagen growth and achieve instant contouring, anti-wrinkle, and even skin tone effects.

Mesotix is an advanced skin reshaping beauty instrument designed for beauty centers. Its principle of treatment involves heating titanium metal to 400 degrees Celsius to stimulate skin cell regeneration and repair through patented TMA technology. This promotes the production of collagen and elastin proteins, improving skin elasticity and smoothness. Mesotix is virtually painless, requiring no topical anesthetics, emits no radiation, and uses only pure natural heat, resulting in fast healing and short downtime. It uses sterile titanium tips as pyramid-shaped needles, which are easy to clean and sterilize during the heating process.

Mesotix treatment is non-invasive, and recovery time is extremely short, making it suitable for customers with any type of skin problem.

Mesotix can be used in conjunction with specially selected essences that can be customized for different skin problems and treatment goals. These essences penetrate deep into the skin for better treatment results, improving a variety of skin problems such as fine lines, wrinkles, dullness, sagging, acne scars, and enlarged pores.It can also improve multiple areas, such as the face, neck, shoulders, hands, arms, and body.
